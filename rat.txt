RATIONAL GAME DESIGN:

-Eliminate unnecessary information

-make things readable, understandable and apparent

-introduce mechanics in an orderly and easily digestible fashion

-and preserve learning and difficulty curves of a game, macro flow (aka)

OBJECTIVES:

	Clearly defined objectives or goals must be present
		High-tier goal: "Save the world from the big bad", guides the entire game flow
		sub-tier goal - build sets of memorable experiences on a moment to moment basis

	Objectives: should be clear and specific
